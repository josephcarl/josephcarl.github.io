There are beers at the lower end of the IBU scale (IBU = International Bitterness Units), indicating more beers are less hoppy. A higher IBU means the beer has a hoppier taste, though this measure is not entirely reliable, as other beer characteristics such as the malt could cover up some of the bitterness in high-IBU beers.

Gearing Up for the Cascadia R Conference

 May 5, 2017
The first ever Cascadia R Conference is less than a month away in Portland, OR, and I am excited to be attending! On top of that, I am thrilled to say my lightning talk proposal, “Building a Personal Website with Blogdown and Github Pages” has been accepted.
